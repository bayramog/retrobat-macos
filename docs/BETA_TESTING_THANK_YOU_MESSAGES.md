## Timing Your Thank You Messages

### Individual Emails
**Send**: Within 24-48 hours of beta closure
**Why**: Show immediate appreciation while their contribution is fresh

### Group Announcements
**Send**: 
- Discord: Immediately when beta closes
- GitHub: When preparing release notes
- Social Media: 1-2 days before public release

### Follow-Up
**Send**: 2-4 weeks after public release
- Share success metrics
- Thank them again for their role
- Invite feedback on public release
- Gauge interest in future betas
